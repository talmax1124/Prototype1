About UNF Career Hub

The UNF Career Hub is designed specifically for University of North Florida students aged 20-24, providing a centralized platform to discover internships, entry-level positions, and career opportunities.

Our platform aggregates opportunities from various sources and provides direct access to UNF's career resources, making your job search more efficient and targeted.
